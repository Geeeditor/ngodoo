NGODOO Development Initiative

INTRODUCTION

At Ngodoo Development Initiative, we aim to ensure a more equitable, sustainable future where women, children, and youth are respected through sustainable socio-political, economic, and environmental development. We address critical global challenges via Education, Environmental sustainability, Gender inclusion, Human Health, and Good Governance.

As an organization that prioritizes the positive transformation of the lives of women, youth, and marginalized communities, we are strategically positioned in a location that enables us to understand unique challenges faced by local communities and organize sensitization campaigns on critical issues at the grassroots level. We have a track record of positively impacting the life of the children through our annual Girltech program and school support tour, our periodic workshops on women empowerment and leadership as well as partnering with other organizations to organize youth-led climate action and awareness campaigns in Nigeria. We collaborate with local communities, partner with like-minded organizations, and harness the power of innovation to drive positive change.

Navigate through our pages, learn about our mission, and discover the thousands of ways you can join us in making a difference. Together, we can create positive change and build a sustainable future for all.

Objectives

  1. To engage both the public and private sector to combat health challenges especially. Create awareness about the relevance of healthy environment and routine check-ups in our communities
  2. Enlighted the young people on the importance of being involved in political processess
  3. To enlighten young people on the need to build self worth through mentorship and skill acquisition
  4. To create an enabling environment to develop skill sets that are useful to the society and oneself. To aid access to employement for youths
  5. To ensure effective participation of the youth congress in international youth activities.
  6. To create awareness on the importance of a healthier environment and carrying out activities to better the condition of our immediate environment.
  7. To serve as a training ground for the youths as future leaders of the country.
  8. To ensure proper mainstreaming of the youths in vital processes of governance, policy formulation, implementation and nation building.
  9. To enhance the promotion of the bonds of peace and friendship among Nigerian youths and their global counterparts.
