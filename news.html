Empowering the future: Our time is now

"Our time is now" Theme for the International Day of Girl child 2022. 11th October is a day set aside not only to celebrate the girl Child but also to highlight the challenges facing the girl child and proffer solutions. In high spirits, we were today at W.M. Bristow Secondary school Gboko with Rt. Hon. Ngunan Addingi to speak with senior secondary school girls who received us with much excitement, she been a revered alumnus. They were advised to be ambitious, set goals and achieve them. They were encouraged to thrive in school and in other aspects of life. In her remarks, Rt. Hon. Adingi told them to always dream big and work hard to make those dreams come through. To come to a close, we shared sanitary pads, liquid detergent, disinfectants and snacks as our parting gifts. It was indeed a moment of great learning and bonding - Our Time Is Now

Fostering Leadership through Education: Ngodoo Development Initiative and Benue State Youth Parliament Empower Pupils

They say education is the mother of Leadership. On Friday, 10th February, 2023, Ngodoo Development initiative partnered with the Speaker, Benue State Youth Parliament who is currently under going a project to support pupils across the senatorial zones in the state. We visited the RCM primary school, Angir Uyough, Nanev, Kwande LGA, Benue State in the Benue North East senatorial district, where we interacted with the pupils in their respective classes and donated materials like pen, pencils, eraser, note books, socks and sandals. Our next port of call will be schools in the Benue North West and South Senatorial districts respectively.
